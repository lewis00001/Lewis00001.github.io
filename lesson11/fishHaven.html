Serial Pie thief Captured

by Megan A. Wirsch

Something fishy has been happening in Fish Haven. Over the last two months, 28 pies have been stolen after residents placed them on their windowsills to cool. Barbara Jones had five pies stolen in a single day. When interviewed she related, "I placed the first pie in the window to cool and then went to powder my nose, you know. When I returned the pie was gone. I figured I forgot to make it and made another one to set in the window. I guess I ended up doing that all afternoon." 


After weeks with no leads, the Fish Haven town shariff, Ashton Burg, received a tip about a pile of abandoned pie plates near the Pike hiking trail. Several hunters volunteered trail cameras and the location was monitored. After just two days the perpetrator, Adam Vager, was captured. He is currently in a holding cell at the Fish Haven Courthouse.
