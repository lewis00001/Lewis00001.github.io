Fires Rage after Thai Restaurant Opens

by Megan A. Wirsch

Looking out over the town of Soda Springs, columns of smoke are seen near the homes of many residents. Sirens can be heard as the local fire department scrambles to extinguish each blaze. The cause? Outhouse fires.


John Pile, the fire marshal's number two guy, said in an interview, "Fires like these have been rare in the past. I know correlation is not causation, but we didn't start seeing these types of fires until after that thai place opened." That 'thai place', also known as Thai Rush, opened for business on center street just a few weeks ago.


During the last town hall meeting, the town mayor and owner of the Rebuildit Lumber Yard stated, "We have no plans to close the thai restaurant. Business is good for our community." However, the debate continues. We'll need to wait and see how fired up the residents get in the end.
